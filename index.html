Partners Profile

PinkSale

PinkSale is a comprehensive platform designed to empower anyone looking to create their own cryptocurrency tokens and launch token sales effortlessly.Tokens created on PinkSale undergo a thorough verification process to ensure quality and authenticity. Once verified, these tokens are officially listed and published on popular explorer websites, providing enhanced visibility and credibility for projects of all scales.

BitMart

BitMart is a leading centralized cryptocurrency exchange offering a wide range of trading and investment services to a global community. With a user base exceeding 9 million, BitMart provides a secure and user-friendly platform for buying, selling, and managing digital assets. Its innovative tools and features are designed to cater to both novice and experienced traders, making it a go-to destination for crypto enthusiasts worldwide.

GemPad

GemPad is an innovative multi-chain decentralized launchpad built to revolutionize the way token funding rounds are conducted. Designed with security and transparency at its core, GemPad offers project owners a reliable platform to launch and manage fundraising campaigns for their tokens.By supporting multiple blockchain networks, GemPad ensures seamless compatibility and accessibility for a diverse range of projects.

MEXC

Founded in 2018, MEXC has established itself as a premier cryptocurrency exchange renowned for its high-performance platform and advanced mega-transaction matching technology. Its cutting-edge infrastructure ensures seamless and efficient trading for users worldwide.The team behind MEXC comprises some of the earliest innovators and pioneers in the fields of financial and blockchain technology.

KuCoin

KuCoin envisions a future where technological advancements redefine how value is created and shared across the globe. The platform is committed to driving innovation and empowering individuals to explore the limitless possibilities of blockchain technology. With its belief in the transformative power of crypto, KuCoin strives to make digital assets accessible to everyone.
